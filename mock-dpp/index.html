Circularity & End-of-Life

Recycler

Dismantling Instructions

Rock mineral wool batts can be removed intact from cavity walls, pitched roofs, and suspended ceilings. Use standard PPE. Separate from vapor barriers and fixings before processing.

Recyclability Assessment

Material is 98% recyclable via re-melting in cupola furnace. Binder content (5%) is thermally decomposed. No hazardous residues.

Sortability Index: A (easily sortable) · Separability Rating: 4/5

Recycling Pathway: Collection → Sorting → Shredding → Re-melting → New mineral wool production. Closed-loop at Knauf Insulation facilities.
